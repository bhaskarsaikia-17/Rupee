# Privacy Policy for Rupee

**Last updated:** January 09, 2026

At **Rupee**, we prioritize your privacy and are committed to protecting it. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your data when you use our mobile application.

## 1. Data Collection and Storage

**Rupee is an offline-first application.**

*   **No Cloud Sync**: We do not store your financial data, transaction history, or personal information on external servers.
*   **Local Storage**: All your data is stored securely on your device's local storage. You have full control over your data.
*   **Data Erasure**: If you uninstall the application or clear its data, your information will be permanently deleted from the device. We cannot recover this data as we do not hold a copy.

## 2. Permissions We Request

To provide you with the full experience, Rupee may request the following permissions:

*   **Camera**: Required for the receipt scanning feature. Images processed are done so locally on your device and are not uploaded to any server.
*   **Notification Access**: Required for the automated expense tracking feature (if enabled). We strictly process transaction-related notifications locally to automate expense logging. Your personal messages (SMS, WhatsApp, etc.) are **never** read, stored, or transmitted.
*   **Storage/Photos**: Required if you wish to attach images to your transactions or save generated reports (PDF/CSV) to your device.
